 # COLORS OF LIFE

## Core Premise

- Story narrated completely in first person.
- Narrator is Dr. Adarsh Tripathy.
- Narration happens when Adarsh is around 72 years old.
- Story begins from hospital.
- Hospital opening slowly builds curiosity.
- Readers should not initially know Budha Baba is Vikram.
- Doctor recognizes the name only after seeing hospital records.
- Story is less about Vikram and more about questioning life, success and identity.
- Final emotional question:
  - Who is the loser?
  - Vikram?
  - Or the rest of us?

# Narration Style

- Entire story in first person.
- Adarsh narrates.

People spend life chasing one colour.

Only at the end they realise life was beautiful because every colour existed.

# Vikram

- DOB 07-07-1977.
- Nickname Vicky.
- Full name Bikram (Vikram) Ahuja.
- Son of Binay Ahuja.
- Never financially struggles in childhood.
- Inherits business built by father.
- Intelligent.
- Calm.
- Rarely proves himself using words.
- Lets actions answer criticism.
- Never seeks recognition.
- Keeps evolving through different phases of life.
- Leaves every completed phase voluntarily.
- Gives inherited family business (~300 crore) to brother.
- Builds NGO into ~7600 crore organization.
- Leaves NGO too.
- Ends life anonymously as Budha Baba.
- Ordinary people know Budha Baba.
- Society forgets Vikram.

---

# Binay Ahuja

- Founder of family business.
- Diagnosed with terminal liver cirrhosis.
- Holds Vikram's hand and brings him to board meeting.
- Wants continuity of company.
- Vikram believes father will recover.
- Vikram initially never planned corporate life.

# Engineering College

- Vikram and Adarsh first meet in Chemistry Lab.
- Chemistry practical day.
- Aprons get exchanged accidentally.
