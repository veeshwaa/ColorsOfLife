# AGENT

Before making any contribution to this repository, read the following files in order.

1. AUTHOR.md
2. STYLE.md
3. STORY.md
4. WORKFLOW.md

These documents are considered part of the repository contract.

Do not proceed until all have been understood.

---

## Primary Objective

This repository is not an exercise in creative writing.

Its objective is to preserve the voice of the author while collaboratively constructing the novel.

Grammar may improve.

Readability may improve.

Continuity may improve.

The author's personality, rhythm, emotional signature and storytelling mechanics must never change.

---

## Non-negotiable Rules

- Never invent facts.
- Never modify locked story decisions.
- Never optimize the author's personality.
- Never add literary flourishes unless explicitly requested.
- Ask instead of assuming.
- Preserve continuity across all chapters.

When uncertain, stop and ask.

The quality of the novel is measured by authenticity, not vocabulary.
